About SUNBIC

SUNBIC Univeristy is a private university in Indonesia. The main campus is located at Kemanggisan, Jakarta. It has other several campuses located in Jakara, South Tangerang and Bandung.

SUNBIC University has a lot of programs and various kinds of facilities. SUNBIC also has a lot of recognition such as A accredited and many more that makes SUNBIC the university that is able to foster and empower Indonesia.

Facilities

SUNBIC Square

SUNBIC Square is the dormiteries provided by SUNBIC that is located close to the campus. It has various types of room that the undergraduates can choose from.

SUNBIC Shuttle Bus

SUNBIC University provides shuttle bus that have a scheduled trip between SUNBIC Square and the campus.

SUNBIC Library

SUNBIC University have an amazing and complete library on each of the campuses. In addition, SUNBIC also have an online library for sunbician.
